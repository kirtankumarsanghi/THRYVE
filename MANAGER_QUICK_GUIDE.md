## 🎨 Color Coding System

- 🟣 **Purple**: Primary actions, navigation, team goals
- 🟢 **Green/Emerald**: Success, on track, completed, approvals
- 🟠 **Orange**: Warnings, at risk, pending, deadlines
- 🔴 **Red**: Errors, overdue, critical issues
- 🔵 **Blue**: Information, meetings, calendar events
